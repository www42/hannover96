Create a new Azure AD Tenant

Important! Telephone number is required.

  1. Go to https://account.azure.com/organization. Fill in the form.

  2. Remember your user account (e.g. Albert@AEinsteinAG.onmicrosoft.com) and your password!

  3. Telephone number has to be correct!

  4. After completion you will be offered an Azure Free Account - Don't use it! Close your browser!

Congratulation! You have created a new Azure AD tenant.
